Instructions for BASK Simulation (with AWGN + Rayleigh)

  • Note: Use the input fields to enter the SNR (in dB), number of multi-paths, excess delay (in ms), bit rate (in bps), and the symbol duration (in ms)
  • Step 1: Click 'Generate BASK Message' to generate the input BASK modulated signal
  • Step 2 & 3: Click 'Generate Multi-path for Rayleigh' and 'ADD AWGN Noise' to simulate fading and add noise
  • Step 4: Click 'LTI Output' to generate the output after convolution with the channel
  • Step 5: Click 'Plot BER vs SNR' to compare BER with and without Rayleigh fading
