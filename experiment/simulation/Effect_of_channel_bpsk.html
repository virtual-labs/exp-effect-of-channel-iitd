Instructions for BPSK Simulation (with AWGN + Rayleigh)

  • Step 1: Click 'Generate Message', 'Generate Carrier', and 'Generate BASK Signal' to generate the input, a 20 Hz carrier, and the modulated signal, respectively
  • Step 2: Click 'Generate Multi-path for Rayleigh' and 'ADD AWGN Noise' to simulate fading and add noise
  • Step 3: Click 'Plot BER vs SNR' to compare BER with and without Rayleigh
